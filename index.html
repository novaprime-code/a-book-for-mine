letter 01
your midnight, my evening

My love,

Every night, when the clock strikes twelve in Nepal, you stay — even though the stars are already telling you to sleep.

And at five in the morning, you rise again for your classroom, for your students, for the world you carry so well.

You give me those midnight hours knowing what they cost you. I don't take a single one for granted. 🌙

// your timezone and mine, stitched together every night
— yours, always
letter 01 · continued

I'm the quiet one. The data person — careful, measured, a little too soft sometimes.

And you? You're all fire and curiosity — lighting up about code, about space, about a new idea at midnight like it's the most thrilling thing in the universe.

Watching you get excited about the things I love too? That's my favourite view in any galaxy. 💫

✦ ✧ ✦

letter 02
you lead, I follow — gladly

Here's a secret...

You are strong. You decide, you take charge, you know exactly what you want — and you're usually right.

And me? I love being led by you. I love your control, your certainty, the way you pull me along.

You're the strength, I'm the softness. Somehow that's the most perfect balance I've ever known. 🤍

// you: the architect · me: happily yours to direct
— led by you
letter 02 · continued

People think the strong one and the soft one don't fit. They've never seen us.

We don't fight. We don't quarrel. We just... orbit — two bodies held by the same quiet gravity. 🪐

You hold the map. I hold your hand. And honestly? I wouldn't want it any other way.

☾ ✦ ☽

Lead me anywhere, my lady. I'm already following.
